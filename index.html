For the modern singer

Improve your pronunciation of foreign arias with Operapedia. Use IPA text to learn the diction and use Operapedia's exclusive vocal coaching to master the nuance of every song.

Vocal Coaching

Operapedia boasts professionally recorded and designed audio guides prepared by an opera vocal coach. These audio guides are rhythmically timed so that the pronunciation is heard just as it would be sung during a performance, broken down for each line of every aria.

IPA Translations

Along with the audio guides, Operapedia also includes original native text along with the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) translation for each line. Opera singers foreign to the native language of the aria can easily pick up an understanding of the correct pronunciation of the aria through the IPA translation.
